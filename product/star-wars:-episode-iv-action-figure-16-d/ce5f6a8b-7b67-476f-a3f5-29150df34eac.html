Star Wars: Episode IV Action Figure 1/6 Dewback Deluxe Version 37 cm

Rugged reptiles with thick hides, dewbacks are plodding but reliable mounts. When prodded by their riders, they are capable of brief bursts of surprising speed. Revisiting the memorable scene of the Sandtrooper discovering a droid part in the vast Tatooine desert in Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, Sideshow and Hot Toys are thrilled to introduce the extraordinary Dewback Sixth Scale Collectible Figure! The highly-accurate Dewback collectible figure is skillfully developed based on the appearance of the creature in the film. Approximately 80 cm in length, the magnificent collectible features greatly detailed head and body texture, an interchangeable open-mouth head, and a saddle for its rider! This Deluxe Version will exclusively feature an interchangeable closed jaw and right standing leg for more display options! This amazing Dewback 1/6th scale collectible will be stunning masterpiece to add to your Star Wars Collectibles collection!
