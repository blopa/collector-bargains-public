<p>After <strong>Athena HQS +</strong> and <strong>Pegasus HQS +</strong>, a new Bronze Saint completes the diorama and defense lines of the Goddess of Wisdom: here is <strong>Andromeda HQS + by Tsume!</strong></p>
<p><strong>Andromeda Shun</strong> is seen here in a defensive position equipped with the <strong>Andromeda</strong> Chain, holding in his left hand its spherical defensive part and in his right hand its pointed offensive part. He also has interchangeable arms / effects showing him deploying his Cosmo energy in translucent purple resin, energy that the Saint of immense potential and exponential power will only deploy as a last resort. Adorned with light reflections, his varnished armor enhances his pale complexion and contrasts with his long green hair covered by his pink and silver helmet. The escaping hairlocks highlight the hopeful green gaze of the benevolent and peaceful Saint, while his expression does not deny the possibility of a devastating attack if that is the only choice he has.</p>
<p>Behind him is the metallic gray <strong>Andromeda</strong> Pandora Box, as indicated by the bust of the princess shown in profile. The emblems on the other walls of the box refer to chivalry and the cosmos. One of the pillars of the <strong>Athena</strong> Temple collapsed behind the formidable Saint, justifying his defensive position. Looking at the ground of the Sanctuary, we can then distinguish the <strong>Andromeda</strong> constellation engraved in stone, while we find at the front of the base the emblem of the sacrificed princess as well as her name written in ancient Greek as on antic temples. The front of the base is interchangeable, allowing the Andromeda plate to appear or not on the stone floor. Equipped with an additional platform to elevate him, the Bronze Saint is intended to take place in the diorama composed of Athena and her defending Saints, ready to defend the great Protector of the Sanctuary!</p>
