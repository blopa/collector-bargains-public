<p><strong>PEGASUS:</strong></p>
<p>After his training in Greece, Seiya became the Pegasus Saint. This MUB represents him in the original version of his Armor, superbly painted and with metallic effects. In order to symbolize the constellation of Pegasus, the Pandora Boxis also present, surrounded by a Pegasus of Cosmos, sign of Seiya's Cosmo-energy.</p>
<p><strong>SANCTUARY:</strong></p>
<p>Cult arc of the anime, the Sanctuary represents a real test for the Bronze Saints. Opposed to the Grand Pope and the Gold Saints, they have 12 hours to save Athena and will have to surpass themselves to succeed!<br /><br />The entire MUB base describes this fight. The 12 houses are present there, extremely detailed thanks to the new Micron by Tsume printing process (see below). On the back of the statue, the Clock is faithfully sculpted, counting down the hours. The painting of this base also shows us the passage of time, being vivid in the first houses and darkening as the hours pass towards the Palace of the Grand Pope.</p>
<p>Different LED sequences will allow you to relive the battle of the sanctuary, simulating the fight of our Heroes in rhythm with the Clock.</p>
<p><strong>ADDITIONAL PARTS:</strong></p>
<p>You can thus place a Pegasus wing on Seiya's back. This, located in the extension of Cosmos' Pegasus represents the constellation's transition to Armor.</p>
<p>A Gold arrow can also be displayed on Seiya's back. It symbolizes the Saints' race against time. This arrow, planted in her heart, will kill Athena if our Heroes do not manage to defeat their enemies in time!</p>
<p>Finally, two different logos can be placed on the bust: One in Greek column color and the second in bronze color.</p>
<p><strong>SYSTEME LED</strong></p>
<p>In addition to the LEDs present on the Cosmos Pegasus and the base, several LED sequences are possible on the houses and the Clock:</p>
<p>1) Full Led : static lighting<br />2) Story : Relive the battle of the sanctuary thanks to a countdown from the Clock and the houses representing the battles of the Bronze Saints.<br />3) My Zodiacal Sign : Light up only the house of your choice and the corresponding symbol on the Clock.</p>
<p><strong>INNOVATION:</strong></p>
<p>We have brought something new to eye treatment. Seiya's eyes are made of transparent resin, allowing us to bring an unprecedented depth and shine to our works. The rendering is intended to be much more realistic and lively. This technology, already used on Naruto MUB, will be brought to our other creations depending on the scale.</p>
